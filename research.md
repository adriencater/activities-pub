### Time and duration

- Museum, gallery, or art space
	- Exhibition
		- Period start and end dates
			- opening days
				- opening hours
		- associated Vernissage event
			- date and time
		- associated Artist talk
			- data and time

- Performing arts centre
	- Danse performance
		- date and time
		- date and time
		- date and time

- Public art installation
	- Period start and end dates
		- accessible 24/7
	- Inaguration event
		- date and time

- Cinema
	- Film
		- Version (original/dubbed)
			- date and time
			- date and time

#### Descriptors
- punctual (quantitative, one or more)
	- date and time (with duration < 1 day)
- ongoing / intermittent (flux, range)
	- date range
		- time range
- recurrence, patterns, exceptions
